hackNY Fall 2015

In this game, there is a central cube. every side of the cube is a different color. You can only see 3 sides of the cube at once. Colored spheres matching with one side of the cube moves from either of the four directions (up, down, right, left) towards the cube. The player must rotate the cube to the colored side that corresponds with the color of the sphere. Players accumulate points by correctly matching the side with the sphere before the sphere hits the cube.
